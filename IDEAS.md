# KidZone Adventure — Feature Ideas

Brainstormed ideas for future development, grouped by category.

---

## Gameplay & Engagement

- **Streak bonus** — 3 correct in a row = bonus star, keeps kids motivated
- **Daily challenge** — one special game per day, highlighted on home screen
- **Hint button** — kids can tap for a clue (costs 1 star), reduces frustration for younger ones
- **2-player mode** — pass-the-phone turn-based, two kids compete on same device
- **Speed bonus** — answer in first 3 seconds = extra point, rewards quick thinkers

---

## New Game Types

- **Memory match** — flip cards to find pairs (animals, flags, shapes)
- **Drag & drop sorting** — sort items into categories (fruits/veggies, big/small)
- **Trace letters** — finger-trace A-Z on canvas, good for 3–5 year olds
- **Word builder** — arrange scrambled letters to spell a word
- **Story math** — "You have 3 apples, you eat 1..." narrative word problems

---

## Progression & Motivation

- **Avatar/pet system** — earn coins, buy accessories for a character
- **Achievement badges** — "First 3-star!", "10 games played", "Speed demon"
- **Learning map** — visual world map showing which countries/capitals unlocked
- **Weekly report card** — summary parents can see: games played, accuracy %

---

## UX & Polish

- **Sound effects & music** — correct/wrong sounds, background music toggle
- **Offline PWA** — installable on home screen, works without internet
- **Vietnamese language toggle** — question text in English or Vietnamese
- **Dyslexia-friendly font option** — OpenDyslexic font toggle in settings
- **Dark mode** — easier on eyes for evening sessions

---

## Parent Features

- **PIN-locked settings** — parents set age group, timer can't be changed by kids
- **Play history log** — which games, how many correct, timestamp
- **Custom timer** — parents input exact minutes instead of fixed 15/20/30
